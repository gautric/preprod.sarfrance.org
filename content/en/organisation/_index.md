The Society in France of the Sons of the American Revolution is the first state society outside the United States of the National Society of the Sons of the American Revolution (NSSAR).

## Purpose of the Society

- To cherish and preserve the memory of those who, during the American Revolution, worked to establish the independence of the American people.
- To foster cordial relations among their descendants.
- To encourage historical research relating to the American War of Independence.
- To maintain the bonds of friendship formed between the French and American peoples at the birth of the American nation.

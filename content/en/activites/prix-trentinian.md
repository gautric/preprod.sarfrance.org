## "Jacques de Trentinian" Award

To encourage historical research and strengthen public interest in the history of the American War of Independence, the Society awards the "Jacques de Trentinian" Prize.

This prize rewards academic research (thesis or dissertation) on the American War of Independence or Franco-American relations of that era.

Maintaining this prize is essential to the Society's mission as an organization recognized as being of public benefit.

The prize is named after [Jacques de Trentinian](/en/histoire/histoire-de-sar-france/), Historian of the Society, whose role is described in the history of SAR France.

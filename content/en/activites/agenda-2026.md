## Our Events

SAR France regularly organizes conferences, visits in Paris and throughout France, as well as gatherings among its members.

Members are also invited to attend ceremonies honoring French and American combatants (Arc de Triomphe, cemeteries of the two World Wars, individual or collective graves of those who fell during the American War of Independence) and to participate in study trips to the United States, in order to maintain the bonds of friendship forged between the French and American peoples at the birth of the American nation.

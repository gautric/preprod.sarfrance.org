## Our Events

SAR France regularly organizes conferences and visits in Paris and across France, as well as gatherings among its members.

Members are also invited to participate in ceremonies honoring French and American combatants (Arc de Triomphe, cemeteries of both World Wars, individual or collective graves of those who died in the American War of Independence), as well as study trips to the United States to maintain the bonds of friendship forged between the French and American peoples at the birth of the American nation.

See our [agenda](/en/activites/agenda-2026/) for upcoming events.

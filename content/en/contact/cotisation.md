## Online Payment

You can pay your membership dues, make a donation to the Society, or pay for participation in one of our activities via the secure Payasso service using the following link:

**[https://www.payasso.fr/revolution-americaine/cotisation](https://www.payasso.fr/revolution-americaine/cotisation)**

## Amounts

- **Annual membership dues**: {{< param "cotisation" >}} €
- **Chancellery fees** (first membership): {{< param "chancellerie" >}} €
- **First registration** (chancellery + dues): {{< param "premiere_inscription" >}} €
- **Reduced dues** (under 35 and foreign residents): {{< param "reduction_pct" >}}% reduction

## Make a Donation to SAR France

SAR France, an association recognized as being of public benefit, is authorized to accept donations to support its activities in the areas defined by its bylaws.

## Tax Benefit

SAR France is an association recognized as being of public benefit. As such, a tax reduction of {{< param "avantage_fiscal_pct" >}}% of the amount of your dues or donations is granted to French taxpayers.

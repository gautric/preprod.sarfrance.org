## Antilles

### 1. Guadeloupe Regiment

**Colonels:**
1. Louis Le Gardeur de Repentigny, 6 November 1775
2. Louis Marie de Martelly, 10 June 1780

**Operations:**
- 1779: Siege of Savannah

### 2. Martinique Regiment

**Colonels:**
1. Christophe Cortasse de Sablonet, 18 August 1772
2. Alexandre Jean Jacques Bernard, Count de Seguins, 24 October 1779, killed by a British cannonball on the Vengeur on 17 April 1780 during the Guichen-Rodney naval battle
3. Louis Le Gardeur de Repentigny, 20 June 1780

**Operations:**
- 1778: Capture of Dominica
- 1779: Capture of Saint Vincent
- 1779: Capture of Grenada
- 1779: Siege of Savannah
- 1780: Guichen-Rodney naval battle, Dominica Channel

### 3. Cap-Français Regiment

**Colonels:**
1. François de Reynaud de Villevert, 18 August 1772
2. Louis Auguste, Count de Sabran, 5 January 1780

**Operations:**
- 1779: Siege of Savannah
- 1782: Naval battle between HMS Santa Margarita and the Intrépide off the American coast (28 July)

### 4. Port-au-Prince Regiment

**Colonel:**
- Joseph de Gripière de Moncroc chevalier de Laval, 18 August 1772 to 25 July 1788

**Operations:**
- 1779: Siege of Savannah

### 5. Saint-Domingue Grenadier Volunteers Corps and Chasseurs-Volontaires of Free People of Colour of Saint-Domingue

**Colonel:**
- Laurent François Lenoir de Rouvray, 12 March 1779

**Operations:**
- 1779: Siege of Savannah

See the detailed article: [The Chasseurs Volontaires de Saint-Domingue](/en/histoire/antilles/chasseurs-volontaires/)

### 6. Bouillé Volunteers Corps

**Commanding Captain:**
- Jean Hippolyte Duboulet, 19 June 1781

### 7. First Legion of Foreign Marine Volunteers

**Commanding Captain:**
- François de Baussancourt

**Operations:**
- 1779: Capture of Saint Vincent
- 1779: Capture of Grenada

## Indian Ocean

### 8. Île de France Regiment

**Colonels:**
1. Joseph Bigeard de Saint Maurice, 9 October 1777
2. Poncin Duchemin de Chenneville, 1<sup>st</sup> January 1781
3. David Charpentier de Cossigny, 17 February 1782

**Operations:**
- 1782–1783: The regiment played a considerable part in the war in India and participated in most of the battles fought by the Bailli de Suffren.

## India

### 9. Pondicherry Regiment

**Colonels:**
1. Siméon Louis Claude Rousselet Desauvergnes, 25 October 1776
2. Louis Alexandre, Baron Dalbignac, 22 August 1780
3. Thomas Count de Conway, 3 March 1781

**Operations:**
- 1778: Defence of Mahé and Pondicherry
- 1782: Capture of Cuddalore and siege of Trincomalee
- 1783: Defence of Cuddalore

### 10. Third Legion of Foreign Marine Volunteers

**Colonels:**
1. Pierre Duchemin, 4 March 1779
2. Antoine Jean Viscount d'Agoult, 1782

**Operations:**
- 1782: Battle of Porto Novo
- 1782–1783: Battle of Cuddalore
- 1783: Battle of Negapatam

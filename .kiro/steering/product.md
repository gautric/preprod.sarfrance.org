# Product: SAR France Website

SAR France (Société en France des Fils de la Révolution Américaine) is the French chapter of the Sons of the American Revolution (NSSAR). This is their institutional website.

## Purpose
- Present the organization, its history, and its mission to preserve the memory of the Franco-American alliance during the American War of Independence
- Publish an agenda of events (conferences, commemorations, visits, NSSAR congresses)
- Provide a biographical dictionary, publications, and historical chronology
- Allow members and the public to contact the organization, join, pay dues, or make donations

## Audience
- Members and prospective members of SAR France
- History enthusiasts interested in the Franco-American alliance
- The general French-speaking public

## Language
All content is in French. The site language code is `fr-FR`. All content files, commit messages, and UI labels should be written in French.

## Domain
- Production: `sarfrance.org`
- Preproduction: `preprod.sarfrance.org`
